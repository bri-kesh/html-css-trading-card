Dog

"A dog's nose print is as unique as a human fingerprint."

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 10–13 years
  • Habitat: Domestic, worldwide
  • Diet: Omnivore
  • Origin: Domesticated ~15,000 years ago

Dogs are the oldest domesticated animals and humanity's most loyal companions. Descended from wolves, they've been bred into hundreds of shapes, sizes, and roles — from herding sheep to guiding the blind to simply being a best friend on the couch.
